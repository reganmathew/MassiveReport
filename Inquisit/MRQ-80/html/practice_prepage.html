Gist Generation Experiment

Practice Round

You are going to familiarise yourself with this experiment in 3 practice trials. (Scroll down for more details)

In each trial,

  1. You will see a cross at the center of the screen. Please focus your eyes on the cross and do not move your eyes.
  2. In a few moments, an image will flash in front of your eyes, before a visual mask is displayed.
  3. Next, you'll see a descriptor word. Decide whether these descriptor words DO or DO NOT refer to the image you saw, including how confident you feel.

If you are ready to start the practice trials, press [Space] to continue.
